About Engine Labs: Eugene’s Connected Subaru Specialists

At Engine Labs, we keep your Subaru adventurous, reliable, and on the road. We are Eugene, Oregon’s premier all-in-one Subaru specialist facility, seamlessly integrating a massive inventory of specialized engine parts with a full-service diagnostic and repair shop. Whether you need a hard-to-find internal engine component, a complete power plant replacement, or routine factory maintenance, we deliver dealership-level expertise without the dealership hassle.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To provide Northwest Subaru owners with unmatched powertrain reliability and total vehicle care under one roof.

We eliminate the boundary between parts sourcing and mechanical service. By building and stocking our own high-tier components locally, we get your vehicle diagnosed, repaired, and back on the road faster and more affordably than general repair shops.

🛠️ What We Do (Our Interconnected Services)

  • Instant Engine Exchange Program: Don't wait weeks for a rebuild. If we have a remanufactured short block or long block in stock that matches your vehicle, you can exchange your tired or damaged motor directly for a fresh, ready-to-install engine to drastically reduce your downtime.
  • Precision Engine Remanufacturing: We build and supply premium remanufactured short blocks, long blocks, and cylinder heads. Every engine is rebuilt to exacting standards using brand-new timing components and high-grade seals to permanently fix common factory weak points.
  • Comprehensive Parts Inventory: We maintain a deep, live inventory of new, used, and remanufactured internal engine components, saving you weeks of waiting for shipped parts.
  • Full-Service Repair & Installation: Bring your vehicle to us for full engine removal and installation, complex system diagnostics, electrical troubleshooting, or routine factory-scheduled maintenance.
  • Custom Rebuild Services: Already have an engine? Bring your block directly to our bench, and we will completely rebuild and refresh it to peak operating specifications.

🔄 How Our Core Exchange Works

We keep standard Subaru blocks on our shelves so you don’t have to wait weeks for a machine shop. To keep this program running for the Eugene community, we operate on a case-by-case core exchange system:

  • The Swap: We pull your current Subaru engine and immediately install one of our fresh, ready-to-go remanufactured short or long blocks.
  • The Core Evaluation: Your old engine becomes a "core" that our team rebuilds for a future customer.
  • Variable Core Fees: If your old engine block matches the replacement model and is fully rebuildable, there is no extra fee. If you do not have an engine to turn in, or if your block has catastrophic damage (such as a thrown rod that cracked the case), a core charge will apply.
  • Why Fees Vary: Subaru engine types and market parts availability vary drastically. Core charges are calculated individually based on your specific power plant (such as high-demand WRX and XT platforms, EJ257 STI blocks, newer FA20s, or specialized H6 models) and will always be clearly disclosed upfront in your initial estimate.
